Our Certification & Online Programs For 2022

The Computer Engineering Department is established in 1994. The department continuously strives to comply with the emerging technologies in the area of Computer. The department runs undergraduate and post graduate program. Computer department is highly proud of having qualified, dedicated and experienced staff. The staff attempts usage of various methodologies to enhance the understanding of the course to the students in the department. Besides conventional methods of teaching like lectures and tutorials, the staff encourages students to learn concepts of the course through workshops, group discussions, group projects and quiz etc. the faculty in the department is also involved in research in their specialized areas and department supports faculty to expertise the subject.


The department started with an initial intake of 40 students in UG program and during the course of time, now it has been enhanced to 120. In 2011, the department started a PG program with an intake of 18 students and research center with intake of 10 student has also started in the department since 2015-16.


The department is headed by Dr. Pravin Nikumbh. His area of interest includes Mobile and wireless communication, soft computing techniques and optimization. The Head of department has rich experience of working at various level through his academic and industrial career.


The Department is well equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and infrastructural facilities in thrust areas. It has advanced laboratories of Manufacturing, Thermal Engineering, Fluids Engineering, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning, latest tools in the work shop, welding shop and carpentry. It also has seven advanced computing centres with software in Finite Element Analysis and Modeling, Automation etc.

About Us

Lokmanya Tilak College of Engineering, a premier regional engineering college in Navi Mumbai, was established in 1994. It is affiliated to University of Mumbai. It is recognized by the AICTE and also accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The college has clean and beautiful campus at Koparkhairane. The Institute has a solid administrative & faculty support which is a pre-requisite for any successful organization.
